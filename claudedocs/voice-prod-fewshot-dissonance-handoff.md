## The finding — verify these exact lines

The v17 template has two runtime slots, `{example}` (few-shot) and `{annotations}`. The two paths fill
them DIFFERENTLY:

- **Prod** (`content-analysis/pipeline.ts`, ~line 357 `analyzeSongs`): builds `AnalyzeSongInput` with
  only `songId/artist/title/lyrics/audioFeatures/genres` — it never sets `exampleText` or
  `annotationsBlock`. So `content-analysis/song-analysis.ts:237-238` resolves both slots to `""`
  (`input.exampleText ?? ""`). **Prod runs v17 with NO worked example and NO annotations.** Confirm with
  `grep -rn "exampleText:\|annotationsBlock:" src` → zero hits. Same template, same temperature (0.3,
  `song-analysis.ts:137`).
- **Eval/harness** (`scripts/voice-audit/regen.ts:~285`): sets `exampleText =
  renderExemplarBlock(selectExemplars(...))` — 2 leave-one-out golds — plus the song's vote-gated
  `{annotations}`. **The eval runs v17 WITH a clean few-shot voice anchor prod never sends.**

The service was deliberately built to NOT fetch golds itself (`song-analysis.ts:61` "the service NEVER
fetches golds… a caller assembles these" — WP1 safety constraint). The harness caller assembles them;
the prod caller (`pipeline.ts`) does not. The `EXEMPLAR_POOL_KEYS` and the planned "fixed prod pair
(Not Like Us + Pink Pony Club)" exist in `regen.ts`/the design docs — the wiring into the prod pipeline
was apparently never completed (a Session-6 cutover gap). **Confirm whether this is intentional
(token-cost/latency) or an oversight before acting — the user believes it is a gap.**
